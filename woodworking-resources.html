By eheisman
Category: wood

  • Steve Ramsey (YouTube) - Lots of good introductory videos for power tool woodworking
  • New Yankee Workshop (not YouTube)

Books

  • Rex Kruger's Everyday Woodworking

Shops

  • Jensen's Hardwood (web / facebook) - great place to buy hardwood in the Walla Walla Valley.
