Learning Tower

Modified plans from Ana White by removing the arch shape and using mortise and tenon joints instead of the pocket screws. I could have easily done it with the pocket screws and had a great result, but I wanted to challenge myself doing a whole bunch of morises and tenons.

Practiced mortise and tenon joints, used a tenon jig that I made to fit over the fence of my table saw. .

Paint turned out poorly, white house paint shows all kinds of dirt.
