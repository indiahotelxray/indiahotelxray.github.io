Shop Improvements

Sat 13 November 2021
By eheisman
Category: wood

I finally decided to get my woodshop cleaned up and more usable in the winter by dividing it out. Every winter I use a few electric heaters to warm it up each day that I want to use it. While I would like to insulate it and put up solid walls to hang stuff on, I'm starting with cutting the space to heat in half.

Proudly powered by Pelican, which takes great advantage of Python.
