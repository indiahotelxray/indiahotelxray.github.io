My Ham Shack

Circa 2015-early 2023 my hamshack lived in a cabinet in the corner of my living room and consisted of:

  • Icom IC-706 MkIIG
  • LDG Z-100 auto tuner
  • MFJ manual tuner, used for SWR meter and as a switch.
  • WestMountainRadio Rigblaster Plug-and-Play
  • Astron switching power supply

Antennas: - G5RV for HF/6M, currently at around 35 ft off the ground in the middle. - 6m 2 element yagi antenna
