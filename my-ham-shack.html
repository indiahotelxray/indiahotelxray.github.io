My Ham Shack

My home shack:

Installed in a built-in cabinet in my living room is a small shack that I use for HF work.

  • Icom IC-706 MkIIG
  • LDG Z-series auto tuner
  • MFJ manual tuner, used for SWR meter and switch.
  • West Mountain Radio Rig Blaster P&P
  • USB Sound Card
  • Astron switching power supply

Antennas: - Half-size G5RV for HF/6M, currently at around 18 ft off the ground.

Future improvements: - Install 2-element 6m antenna on rotator on a mast over my garage. - 2m and 70cm
